What I bring

Targeted marketing strategy.
Real field knowledge.

The women's cycling market is a growth lever to develop in France. Women represent 30% of urban cyclists and less than 20% of sport cycling, a structural gap that creates real opportunities for organisations with a deep understanding of these audiences.

Since 2020, I have been working directly with this community: its practices, expectations and codes. This field experience directly informs my ability to build relevant strategies and adapted content.

I work in direct collaboration with teams, on missions selected for their coherence with my field of expertise.

Product strategy & launch

In-depth knowledge of the market, products and distribution: bikes, accessories, retail. Supporting brands in their launch strategy, positioning or product creation adapted for women.

03
Consulting & support

Supporting organisations wishing to structure their approach to feminising sport participation. Diagnosis, strategic recommendations, operational implementation.

Outdoor sport & events
Increasing female participation rates in outdoor events.

Trail, cycling, major sporting events. Supporting organisers in building a dedicated strategy to attract and retain women: offer positioning, targeted communication, community partnerships.

Feminising outdoor events requires a deep understanding of barriers to participation, knowledge of active female communities, and an approach that values participants' experience as much as performance.

From a media to an expertise

Beyond My Bike —
where it all
began.

In 2021, I launched Beyond My Bike to cover urban cycling for women at a time when the subject was little covered. This project was a concrete experimentation ground: content production, community building, partnerships, e-commerce, pop-up stores.

This experience gave me the legitimacy to work directly with the players who shape the market — federations, start-ups, associations. BMB remains active and constitutes a reference resource on the subject.
